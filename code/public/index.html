Visual Analytics Lab at Tufts

    This study takes place in 5 parts:

  1. Consent form: review and sign electronically for your protection.

  2. Tutorial video: A short video on how to use the Voyager data visualization tool.

  3. Practice question: An example of the experiment questions using Voyager.

  4. Experiment questions:Using Voyager for data visualization, you will be asked questions about the data. Please answer these questions to the best of your ability.

  5. Post-experiment questionnaire:a brief survey about your experience.

It is very important that you do not click the "back" button or refresh during your session. Doing so may result in your session being lost.
